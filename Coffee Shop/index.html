Current Food System

The current food system is characterized by large-scale industrial agriculture, global supply chains, and high resource consumption.

  • Widespread use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides
  • High dependency on fossil fuels for transportation and processing
  • Loss of biodiversity in crops and ecosystems
  • Food waste at various stages of production and consumption
  • Issues of food insecurity and unequal distribution

What we have on the menu

Only a few items, but they're really good!

Indulge in the rich flavors of our Walnut Soy Milk Coffee. Made with freshly roasted walnuts and handcrafted soy milk, this delightful beverage offers a perfect blend of nuttiness and creaminess.

Experience the delicate balance of flavors in our Matcha Ice Cream. Crafted from premium matcha leaves, this ice cream brings together the earthy essence of matcha with the smoothness of creamy goodness.

Delight your taste buds with our Brownie Cookies. These irresistible treats combine the chewiness of brownies with the crunchiness of cookies, offering a divine medley of textures and flavors.

Savor the tropical goodness of our Mango Float. Made with luscious mangoes and layers of creamy goodness, this dessert provides a refreshing burst of flavors that transport you to paradise.

Energize your day with our Iced Americano. This classic coffee beverage combines bold espresso shots with chilled water, offering a revitalizing and invigorating experience that fuels your senses.
